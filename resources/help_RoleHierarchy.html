Role Hierarchy

Creating Role Hierarchy

You can create a tree view hierarchy simply by dragging and dropping members already chosen for project. To create a hierarchical relation between members, you need to drag a member and to drop it onto its superior member. Hierarchy arrangement rule follows.

  • Members move along when their superior member moves.
  • Members move one level up on the tree where their superior member is deleted.
  • If a member has multiple roles for a project, do not make one role report to the other.

Editing Member

Selecting member name from the role hierarchy pops up Edit window. You can change Role only

Deleting Member

You can delete a member by selecting Delete button on Edit window.
