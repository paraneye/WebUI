Companies

Companies list

All generated companies names are listed. Companies are classified three groups, Owner, Contractor, and Sub-contractor. The companies list become available for the company selection for users and human resources.

Adding New Company

Selecting Add New displays pop up window for adding a new company. Fields denoted with '*' are required fields.

  • Company Name : name of the company
  • Type : company type of the company. Types for selection are Owner, Contractor, and Sub-contractor
  • Address : company address
  • Logo : photo image of company logo. You should choose an image file smaller than 200Kbyte

Editing Company

Selecting Company Name from the companies list pops up Edit window. Administrator only can edit company¡¯s information.

Deleting Company

Administrator only can delete companies. Put a check to company you want to delete and select Delete on the companies list
