Drawing Viewer

Viewing Drawings

To find the drawing you want to see, select CWP number which the drawing is associated with or search with keyword for drawing number. Searched drawings are listed in the form of thumbnail. When you click a thumbnail, a popup window displays the selected drawing image. You can zoom in and zoom out the drawing, and pan in and pan out the drawing on it. Information of the drawing such as drawing number, drawing type, drawing title, drawing description, reference drawing numbers and detail drawing numbers are displayed on the same screen.
